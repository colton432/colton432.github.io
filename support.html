About Budget Buddy

Budget Buddy is a local-first budgeting app for tracking transactions, categories, subscriptions, spending trends, yearly summaries, and budget exports.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

Budget Buddy stores app data locally on your device. The app does not use a cloud backend and does not require a user account.

Does Budget Buddy connect to my bank account?

No. Budget Buddy does not connect to bank accounts. Transactions and subscriptions are entered and managed manually.

What happens if I delete the app?

Since Budget Buddy stores data locally, deleting the app may delete your saved app data from the device. Before deleting the app, export any budget summaries or CSV files you want to keep.

Can I export my budget data?

Yes. Budget Buddy supports exporting budget summaries as PDF previews or CSV files for a selected month, full year, or custom date range.

Does the app support dark mode?

Yes. Budget Buddy supports light mode and dark mode.

Does the app show ads?

Budget Buddy may display mobile banner ads on supported platforms.
