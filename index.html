Track Your Spending Locally

Budget Buddy is a Flutter budgeting app designed to help users manage personal spending directly on their device. Users can add, edit, search, filter, and delete transactions with names, amounts, dates, notes, and categories.

Main Features

  • Add, edit, search, filter, and delete transactions
  • Track transaction names, amounts, dates, notes, and categories
  • Use default categories such as Gas, Food, Rent, Entertainment, Shopping, and Other
  • Create and manage custom spending categories
  • Track recurring subscriptions with monthly or yearly billing
  • Mark subscriptions as active or inactive
  • View upcoming bills and estimated subscription totals
  • See current month spending summaries
  • View category cards, top category, recurring costs, and recent transactions
  • Review category views and transaction history
  • Analyze spending with pie charts, line charts, and monthly trend charts
  • View yearly summaries and month-by-month breakdowns
  • Export budget summaries as PDF previews or CSV files
  • Export by selected month, full year, or custom date range
  • Use light mode or dark mode

Private by Default

Budget Buddy stores data locally on the user’s device using local storage. The app does not require a cloud account, does not use a cloud backend, and does not connect to bank accounts.

Built for Simple Budgeting

Budget Buddy is made for users who want a straightforward way to track spending manually, monitor subscriptions, understand spending habits, and export summaries when needed.
