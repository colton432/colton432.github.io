Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Budget Buddy handles user data.

Data Storage

Budget Buddy stores budgeting data locally on your device using local device storage. This may include transactions, categories, notes, subscription information, billing dates, spending summaries, and app preferences.

Budget Buddy does not use a cloud backend for storing your budgeting data.

No Bank Connection

Budget Buddy does not connect to bank accounts, financial institutions, credit cards, or payment accounts. All transactions and subscriptions are manually entered and managed by the user.

No Account Required

Budget Buddy does not require users to create an account or log in to use the app.

Information We Do Not Collect

Budget Buddy does not collect, sell, or share personal budgeting data through a cloud database.

Budget Buddy does not collect bank login information, bank account numbers, credit card numbers, or financial institution credentials.

Exports

Budget Buddy may allow users to export budget summaries as PDF previews or CSV files. Exported files are created only when the user chooses to export data. Users are responsible for where they save, share, or store exported files.

Advertising

Budget Buddy may display mobile banner advertisements. Third-party advertising providers may use device or advertising-related information to display ads, measure ad performance, and prevent fraud.

Budget Buddy does not send your manually entered budgeting transactions, notes, categories, or subscription details to an advertising provider for cloud storage.

Device Storage and App Deletion

Because Budget Buddy stores data locally, deleting the app may also delete the app’s stored data from your device. Users should export any important summaries or CSV files before deleting the app.

Children’s Privacy

Budget Buddy is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if Budget Buddy adds new features, services, analytics, advertising tools, or data handling practices.
